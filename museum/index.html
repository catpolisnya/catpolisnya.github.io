Welcome to the IDHM.

International Digital History Museum is a project celebrating various quirks of the digital world of ours that are no longer with us or fell out of popular use.

We aim to represent cultural spheres of the internet that aren't just the American online space and to uphold the memory of what once was reigning in the cyberspace.


If you have a suggestion about what could be added to the museum or you have feedback regarding Museum, do not hesitate to contact the museum staff with all necessary information regarding your inquiry.

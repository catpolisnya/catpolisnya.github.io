Hyves

Before Facebook's monopolist reign, there were many other social networking sites around the world. Hyves is one of them.

Operating in the Netherlands, it obviously amassed a giant Dutch userbase, despite being available also in English.
Since opening in 2004, it has gathered over 10 million users (including alt and inactive accounts).

It has closed its doors in 2013.


Status
Hyves itself is non-existent. The only remaining thing is Hyves Games, where you can use your Hyves account to play games.

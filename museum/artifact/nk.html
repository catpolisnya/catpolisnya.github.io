Pan Gąbka - Nasza Klasa

In the era of local social networking sites, the number one website in Poland was Nasza-Klasa that launched in 2006.
Initially starting as a website to find your own classmates, it soon evolved into a national monopoly for everyone. One of the features was an explicit allowance of "fictional accounts" - something Facebook despises because it poisons their data harvesting empire.
Its most popular feature was an on-site currency called "eurogąbki" (eurosponges) that could be used for profile gifts or buying various in-game currencies.

NK has fallen out of use around 2014, and finally closing their doors in 2021.


Status
NK is no longer accessible. There is only a redirect to the site owner's gaming webpage.

Hypercam 2

In the old days of YouTube, it was unheard of to use OBS for recording, and if any editing existed, it was usually done with Windows Movie Maker.
Due to its low level of entry and being free, Hypercam 2 was used by people to record their epic Club Penguin tutorials or Runescape gameplays.

It was mostly recognizable because of its watermark in the top left corner, despite the fact you could simply pay once to get rid of it.


Status
While everyone moved away from using HC2 for recording, you can still download a copy on Internet Archive!
